INVOLVEMENT

Amended Fisheries Code Becomes Law in the Philippines: In February 2015, Republic Act 10654 amended the Philippine Fisheries Code, raising sanctions for illegal fishing to $45 million (PHP) for commercial violators and $2.4 million (PHP) for poachers. The law mandated a Monitoring, Control, and Surveillance system on Philippine fishing vessels to combat illegal activities. These amendments helped the Philippines avoid European Union penalties for insufficient sustainable fishing practices.
