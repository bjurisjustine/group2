INITIATIVES

Oceana is a global non-profit organization focused on ocean conservation.

The organization's main initiative is to promote policies and practices that ensure

the sustainability and health of the world's oceans.

Oceana's initiatives typically include a combination of scientific research,

advocacy, and public outreach.
