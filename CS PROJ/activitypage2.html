Panahon ng Panaon Expedition: Two years after legally protecting the Benham Bank, Oceana launches a pandemic-era expedition to safeguard a crucial coral site in Southern Leyte, including 17 marine protected areas. This time, our focus is on Panaon Island's vital coral reefs, recognized for their diversity by the 50 Reefs Initiative. Led by Marianne Saniano, Danny Ocampo, and Dr. Badi Samaniego, the expedition prioritizes data collection with strict safety measures. Thanks to Oceana, funders, and our partners, including Governor Damian Mercado and Mayors Chong, Gamutan, Estrella Sr., and Salinas. This newsletter celebrates our joint commitment to marine conservation amid global challenges.
